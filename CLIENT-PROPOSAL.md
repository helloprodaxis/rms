## 2. The Restaurant Industry Today — The Real Problems

We have spoken to dozens of restaurant owners across India before designing this product. The same five complaints come up every single time:

### Problem 1 — Too many tools, too many bills.
Most restaurants use one tool for billing (Petpooja, Posist), another for QR ordering (DotPe), another for delivery (Zomato/Swiggy aggregator dashboard), a separate WhatsApp Business number, and pay a freelancer or agency separately for the website. Five vendors, five invoices, five passwords, five places where things can break.

### Problem 2 — Setup fees and lock-ins.
The big SaaS players charge ₹3,000 to ₹15,000 as a one-time setup fee, and then lock you into an annual contract. If you want to leave after three months, you cannot — you've already paid for the year.

### Problem 3 — No real online presence.
Most small and mid-size restaurants either have no website, or have an old WordPress site built five years ago that nobody updates. Customers searching on Google find an empty Justdial listing instead of your restaurant. You lose customers before they walk in.

### Problem 4 — Aggregator dependence.
Zomato and Swiggy take 18% to 30% commission on every order. Restaurants are bleeding margin to platforms they cannot control. Your own customers are not even your own — the aggregator owns the relationship.

### Problem 5 — The technology feels like a burden, not a tool.
Owners are scared of complicated software. Staff resist it. Training takes weeks. Things break at peak hour and nobody picks up the phone. The technology, instead of helping the business, becomes another headache.

**[Your Brand] was built specifically to fix every one of these five problems.**

**Step 3 — Menu.** Your menu opens in their browser — fast, beautiful, with photos of every dish, customer ratings, allergen markers, and clear pricing. They browse like they would on Swiggy, except this is your brand and your relationship.

**Step 5 — Kitchen sees it.** Within two seconds, your kitchen display lights up. The cook starts preparing. Status on the customer's phone changes to "Preparing".

**Step 8 — Bill.** Customer taps "Request Bill" or pays directly from the phone via UPI. The waiter brings the printed GST bill if needed. No waiting, no awkward eye contact, no signal-flagging.

## 8. GST, FSSAI & Indian Compliance — Built In

We have built [Your Brand] to be 100% compliant with Indian regulations from day one. This is not a feature you have to configure — it just works.

### GST Compliance

- Automatic **CGST + SGST** split for intra-state sales.
- **IGST** for inter-state (catering, banquets).
- Configurable GST rates per item — 5% (no ITC) for standard restaurants, 18% (with ITC) for restaurants in hotels with room tariff above ₹7,500, or any other rate as applicable.
- **HSN/SAC codes** stored against every menu item.
- Sequential invoice numbering per financial year, as required by law.
- GSTIN displayed on every bill.
- **One-click GSTR-1 export** in the format your CA can directly upload.
- B2C summary, B2B (with GSTIN) handling, and credit note management.
- E-invoicing flag and integration ready for outlets above the turnover threshold (currently ₹5 crore).

### FSSAI Compliance

- Your FSSAI licence number displayed on every bill.
- FSSAI number visible on the website footer (mandatory).
- Auto-reminder before licence expiry.

### Service Charge — CCPA Compliant

The Central Consumer Protection Authority (CCPA) issued guidelines in 2022 making service charge **strictly optional and not auto-addable** to bills. We have built this correctly:

- Service charge is **off by default**.
- If you choose to enable it, the customer sees a clear **"optional, you may decline"** notice and can remove it before paying.
- This protects you from consumer complaints and legal action.

### Data Protection (DPDP Act 2023)

- Customer phone numbers, emails, and order history are stored with explicit consent.
- One-click data deletion on customer request (mandated by law).
- Audit trail of all access.

### Other regulatory readiness

- Bill format compliant with the **GST Invoice Rules** (Rule 46).
- Discount and refund handling with proper credit notes.
- Tip/gratuity handling separated from service charge.
- Multi-state operation supported (each outlet's GSTIN tracked separately).

**You will not get fined, audited badly, or sued because of how our software handles tax or compliance. That's our promise.**
