FAQ

Quick answers.

How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work?
You pay your first month upfront. If within 30 days you're not satisfied for any reason, we issue a full refund of that month and cancel your account — no questions asked. You don't lose a single rupee on the trial.
Why is Pro highlighted as the recommended plan?
Standard gives you only a static website. Pro is the full Restaurant OS — QR ordering, kitchen display, GST billing, online orders, loyalty, inventory and the premium dynamic website. Most restaurants need everything in Pro from day one.
What's the difference between Standard and Pro websites?
Standard is a static 5-page website — perfect if you only want an online presence. Pro adds dynamic features: photo gallery, customer reviews, online ordering, reservations, loyalty integration, and the entire restaurant operations stack.
Is GST handled correctly?
Yes. Auto CGST/SGST/IGST split, HSN codes, FSSAI on bill, sequential invoicing, one-click GSTR-1 export. CCPA-compliant service charge handling.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No annual contract. First 30 days are money-back guaranteed. After that, if you cancel within 12 months from website go-live, a one-time Website Recovery Fee applies (Rs. 15K/25K/50K) since the website was built free upfront. After 12 months, free cancellation.
Do you really charge 0% commission?
Yes. Zero commission on dine-in and online orders through your site. Only your payment gateway (Razorpay) charges its standard fee — that goes to them, not us.
